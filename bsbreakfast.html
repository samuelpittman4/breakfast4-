Pancakes

By: Samuel Pittman

I make this breakfast because it is quick and I can eat it when I'm on the go.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 2 Cups Milk
  • Pancake batter
  • 2 tbs cinnamon
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

WHAT TO DO:

  1. Mix the 2 cups of milk with about 2-3 cups of pancake batter until creamy
  2. Heat skillet on medium heat while mixing ingredients
  3. Carefully pour batter into shape of choice and sprinkle a pinch of cinnamon in each pancake. Add chocolate chips before flipping.
  4. When side facing down is golden brown flip pancake and sprinkle another pinch of cinnamon onto the pancake
  5. Remove from skillet once both sides are golden brown
