Bacon, Egg, and Cheese Sandwich

By: Morgan Miller

I recently dropped my 9:40am class on Mondays and Wednesdays so now I do not start classes until 12:40pm. This means...I GET TO MAKE BREAKFAST since I no longer have to rush.

INGREDIENTS:

  • Bread
  • Jelly
  • Bacon
  • Oil
  • 1 Egg
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Milk
  • Cheese

WHAT TO DO:

  1. Crack the egg in a bowl
  2. Pour salt, pepper, and milk into the bowl and mix
  3. Pour oil into a skillet and turn the heat up
  4. Place as many strips of bacon as you wish inside of the skillet, flipping them over occassionally
  5. Now, pour the eggs into a pan and put the stove on low
  6. While the bacon and eggs are cooking, place your slices of bread into the toaster oven
  7. Add cheese to your eggs
  8. Once your toast is done, put a spread of jelly on top along with your bacon and eggs
  9. Serve
